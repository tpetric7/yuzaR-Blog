R package reviews {dlookr} diagnose, explore and transform your data

Row data need to be diagnosed for problems, explored for new hypotheses and transformed in order to increase data quality. The {dlookr} package makes these steps fast and easy. It is able to generate automated reports or performe compex operation, like imputing missing values, with simple functions.
